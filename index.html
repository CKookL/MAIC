Welcome to

Mind and Integrity College

Mind and Integrity College, Inc. is one of the most dynamic and most unique campus of the growing institution in the City of Calamba. MAIC has developed its own innovative position as a particularly dynamic and vibrant institution with strong academic links with industry. With over 14 years of service, MAIC offers a fantastic opportunity to learn within a vibrant and dynamic school setting and providing a good quality education for the Senior High School and College Students.

MAIC is also proud to have a qualified teachers whom are LET passers and Master's Degree Holders. Its academic programs will enable students and teachers to develop closer ties with the industry they will eventually enter into, thus giving them a unique head start.
